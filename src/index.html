Median Household Income's Relationship with Heart Disease & Stroke
This is a visualization that is used to illustrate how median income in a county may affect health conditions such as stroke, coronary heart disease, high blood pressure and or high cholesterol. It is not to say that one is indicative of the other or there is a causation amongst correlation. This data was collected by the CDC on county by county basis in 2024.
